What is Lift ?

Liftweb is free and expressive web application framework
that provides developers ability to create secure, scalable, maintainable ,
rich and interactive web applications. Lift is the most powerful and secure framework available today. Liftweb is  designed specifically to leverage
the Scala programming language  and takes View-First approach rather going via the standard Model-View-Controller way.
It was originally created by Sir David Pollak who was dissatisfied with certain aspects of the Ruby on Rails framework.. The initial release of Liftweb was launched on February , 2007 .
It is open source and licensed under Apache 2.0 . license  .
